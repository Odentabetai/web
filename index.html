About France

France is a country located in Western Europe.France is famous for its art and food such as French bread,croissant, and, Escargot.

There are very famous buldings. For example, Louvre Museum,the tower of Effel,and Notre Dame Cathedral. A lot of toursists visit these place.

In addition, France is famous for being an ecologically conscious country.

Tourist spot of France

The tower of Effel

The Eiffel Tower is located in Paris, France and is the symbol of the city.It was built by Gustave Eiffel in 1889 to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution.

Mont Saint-Michel

Mont Saint-Michel is a small island and monastery on the Bay of Saint-Malo on the west coast of France. It is registered as a cultural heritage and is mysterious.

Palace of Versailles

The Palace of Versailles is a palace built by Louis XIV in the 17th century. It is one of the most famous baroque buildings in the world. It is very gorgeous and graceful.
